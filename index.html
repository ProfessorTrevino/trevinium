We took that myth and flipped it on its head.

Treviño Solutions introduces the Trevinium Audio Recorder, the first of a series of three apps — and the first and only app to capture true, raw, stunning 48 kHz audio directly from AirPods and other Bluetooth microphones.

To understand what that means:

  • Most Bluetooth microphones record at 8–16 kHz (phone-call quality).
  • A few reach 24 kHz, but only with heavy processing and artifacts.
  • Some apps fake higher numbers using filters, upsampling, compression, and codecs to claim “studio quality.” These are misleading — they change the numbers after recording at ~16 kHz.
  • No system on Earth has ever produced real, raw 48 kHz audio over Bluetooth.

Until now.
